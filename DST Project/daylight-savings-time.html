3 - Why Should We Abolish Daylight Saving Time?

{insert MLA from DOI)}

This journal seguests there are 3 types of clocks we use. These are sun clock, body clock, and social clock. They propose that these clocks have relationships and defined relatively. So when DST shifts everything it breaks all the ratios between the clocks. They refrance some papers on how even living on the western most areas of time zones can lead to health problems, but I'm not able to verify (2024-10-02 11:34).

Several studies found that the further west people live within a time zone, the more health problems they may experience and the shorter they live on average (Borisenkov, 2011; Gu et al., 2017; VoPham et al., 2018)

roenneberg-et-al-2019-why-should-we-abolish-daylight-saving-time, page 2

Furthermore the authors mention China's 1 unified time zone and how that cause problems, because we live according to the same social. But from what I have indirectly researched Chinese people on the western areas just shift things basically making there own time zones. As in people in the western provinces may eat at a later time for breakfast than eastern coast people (2024-10-02 11:54).

After reading Borisenkov, 2011's abstract, I am unsure of the academic credibility. It seems slightly absurd to be true, but there is the other 2 articles to read. (2024-10-06 17:19)

Some countries (like China) have combined neighboring time zones to make their time zones bigger, so that the sun needs even longer than 1 h to cross the time zone. We live according to the same social clock time within a time zone, but as long as we still can see the natural day (through windows or on our way to or from work or school), our body clocks still follow more or less the time of the sun clock defined by the sun’s east-west path.
